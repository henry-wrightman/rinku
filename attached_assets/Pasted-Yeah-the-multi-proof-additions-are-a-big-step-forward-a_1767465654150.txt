Remaining gaps / tweaks I’d still push you to make
1) Define the exact Merkle-sum hash preimages (domain separation)

Right now the multi-proof structure is precise, but you still need to lock down the hashing rules for:

leaf hash

internal node hash

inclusion of sumWeight in the hash preimage (not just “added as metadata”)

Otherwise two implementations can both be “right” and still disagree on valRootHash. I’d explicitly specify something like:

leafHash = SHA256("rinku:val_leaf:v1" || index || address || blsPubKey || weight)

nodeHash = SHA256("rinku:val_node:v1" || left.hash || left.sumWeight || right.hash || right.sumWeight)

(Use the same “domain separator” rigor you already used for checkpoint IDs.)
