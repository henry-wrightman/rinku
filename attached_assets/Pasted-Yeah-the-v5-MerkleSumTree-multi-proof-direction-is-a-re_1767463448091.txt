3) Small correctness nit: “base64url contains /”

You wrote QR byte-mode justification as “base64url contains -, _, /”. Base64url’s alphabet replaces + and / with - and _ (so / shouldn’t appear in the payload itself).

Not a huge deal (byte-mode is still the safe assumption for QR), but it’s an easy fix that prevents pedants from derailing you.
