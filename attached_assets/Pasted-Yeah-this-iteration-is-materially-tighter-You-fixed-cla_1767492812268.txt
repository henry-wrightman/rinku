To make this coherent, you need to state (and enforce) one of these:

Option A (most practical): “zk-enabled transactions” include an extra zkPubKey + zkSig field inside the tx that is merkle-committed. Validators still check ECDSA for network validity; ZK receipts additionally rely on zkSig that was committed at tx creation time.
